About The Company

GAL Aluminium Extrusion Pvt. Ltd. (GAL)

has been in the aluminium extrusions industry for more than(since) two decades. From humble beginnings in the year 1992 in Ahmednagar when we had set up our first industrial unit, we have grown by leaps and bounds.

Our Story

Gal Aluminium Extrusion has been pioneering aluminium extrusion industry since 1992 & today it is one of the top aluminium extrusion suppliers in India. To cater to the today’s market needs, we have imported advanced production lines and Extrusion Press from Taiwan and also Casting Hot Pot System from China. GAL Group has always committed for quality parameters and fullled that commitments. By sowing seeds of hard work, advancement, innovation and maintaining quality commitments. We have reaped the fruits of being awarded an ISO 9001: 2015, ISO 14001: 2015 and OHSAS 18001:2017certicates.

Vision

Team GAL the men with dark blue and stripped light blue cascaded by the Qualified Engineers and highly experienced technocrats. Capable on doing anything possible with extrusion process. We proudly claim “Aluminium Extrusion Made Easy” Established in 1991, GAL Aluminium is today an ISO 9001 company with a wide range of products catering to the needs of customers worldwide. Foreseeing the vital role that Aluminium would play in the future, we invested in technology and quality assurance, working towards a position of undisputed leadership in Aluminium Extrusion.
